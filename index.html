Cake recipes

Cake is a confectionery product consisting of several cakes impregnated with cream or jam. On top, the cake is usually decorated with cream, glaze or fruit.

There are also unsweetened varieties of cakes (for example, liver cake), while the name "cake" indicates rather the design of the dish: laying the ingredients in layers with possible subsequent decoration of the upper layer. In an example with a liver cake: toasted cake of liver mince with a layer of mayonnaise, decorated with greenery on top.
